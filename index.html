My Journey So Far

Hello, I'm Jitendra Raghav.

I am a 20-year-old aspiring developer from the small village of Sultanpur Bilauni in Uttar Pradesh, India. My world changed when I discovered coding; it felt like unlocking a superpower that could bring ideas to life. I spend every spare moment learning, driven by a deep passion to build applications that can solve real-world problems.

Currently, my gateway to this world is an old HP Pavilion G4 laptop. While I am incredibly grateful for it, its age and limitations are now a major roadblock. It struggles to run the modern development tools, compilers, and emulators that are essential for me to grow my skills and build more complex projects.

The Goal: A Tool for a Dream

To take the next leap in my journey, I need a more capable machine. My goal is to raise $342 to purchase a reliable, second-hand laptop that can handle the demands of modern coding.

Your small contribution can make a huge difference. The concept is simple: if enough people believe in this dream, even a donation as small as $0.10 can help me reach the finish line. Every single cent brings me closer to the tool I need to build a better future.
